### Project
This week, we started working on the project with the goal of proving the following Theorem 1 of <a href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/quant-ph/0412001">SIC-POVMs and the extended Clifford group (D. M. Appleby, 2005)</a> for the special case where $d$ is an odd prime. 
In summary, the theorem states that the projective Clifford group on one $d$-dimensional qubit is isomorpic to the affine symplectic group over $\mathbb{Z}_d$:

$$ \mathrm{C}(d) / \mathrm{I}(d) \simeq \mathrm{Sp}(2, \mathbb{Z}_d) \ltimes \mathbb{Z}_d.$$

Find the pre-digested proof a blueprint here at <a href="https://marozols.github.io/clifford-project/">https://marozols.github.io/clifford-project/</a>
